Latest book

Dark Ages Online

Release date: 02/13/26
Series: Dark Ages Online: Family
Genre: LitRPG

Luke enters the VR game, Dark Ages Online, with his mother and sister. They explore the virtual world, make new friends and explore their abilities. Between dungeon raids and battling bosses, they level their characters and outfit them with epic gear.

Luke and his family dove into the game in honor of their late father and husband. It was intended to be a memorial to him, but everything changed when they discovered his avatar. They have a second chance with him but are thrust in the middle of a powerful group trying to take it all away.

Luke and his family have to band together, or they will lose him again.
